title: 'GitLab'
description: 'Sync your docs with a GitLab repo'
---

<Check>
  GitLab is available on the [growth plan](https://mintlify.com/pricing).
</Check>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your repository's Project ID">
    Navigate to your project's `Settings` > `General` and find the Project ID.
    <img src="/images/gitlab/gitlab-project-id.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a project private token">
    1. Navigate to your project's `Settings` > `Access Tokens`.
    2. Select `Add new token` and add a token name and choose `read_api` for the scopes. If you have a private repo, you must set the role as `Reporter`.
    3. Finally click `Create project access token` and copy the token.
  </Step>
</Steps>
