---
title: "MacOS Install"
description: "Install Repacket on your MacOS computer."
---

<Note>Repacket can be installed and uninstalled silently using a [compatible MDM](/setup/mdm/guides).</Note>
<Warning>You need administrator privileges to install Repacket. Our [MDM deployment guides](/setup/mdm/guides) may offer a workaround.</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the MacOS agent">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Repacket Agent (MacOS)" icon="apple" href="https://repacket-installers.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/Repacket.pkg" />
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the agent">
    ![](/images/macos-install.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow the proxy configuration">
    This step allows the Repacket agent to analyze your traffic on the device.

    Click the "Allow" prompt

    ![](/images/approve-proxy.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve the network extension">
    Repacket uses a MacOS network extension on MacOS to handle routing traffic to our agent.
  
    Click the "Open System Settings" prompt to enable our network extension.

    <Warning>
      If you lost this menu, you can find it in System Settings > General > Login Items & Extensions. 

      Scroll down to "Network Extensions" and click the <Icon icon="circle-info" color="grey" /> icon.
    </Warning>

    ![](/images/approve-network-extension.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable extension">
    Finally, toggle our Network Extension to enabled.

    ![](/images/settings-network-extension.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Login to Repacket">
    When you browse to any web page, you will be asked to login to Repacket.

    ![](/images/repacket-login.png)

    ![](https://dashboard.mintlify.com/repacket/repacket/editor/images/login-page.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
